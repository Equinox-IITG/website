Propose a Project

Share your astronomical vision with us. Whether it's groundbreaking research, engaging public outreach, or innovative technological development, we're excited to explore the cosmos together. Your ideas can help illuminate the path forward in astronomy.

Project Proposal Guidelines

  • Projects should directly align with the core themes of **astronomy, astrophysics, or broader space sciences**.
  • Ensure the project is **feasible** given typical resources and a realistic timeline.
  • Clearly articulate your project's **objectives** and anticipated **outcomes**.
  • Specify any required **equipment, software, datasets, or collaborative expertise**.
  • Projects with **educational and public outreach** components are highly encouraged and will receive priority.
